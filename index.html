About me

I am an economist and policy analyst with experience in migration, labour markets, inequality, applied econometrics and public policy. My work explores how migration, displacement and macroeconomic shocks affect social protection, labour outcomes and development.

I have worked with international organisations such as the OECD and the Inter-American Development Bank, and with government institutions in Colombia. My technical background includes econometrics, machine learning, forecasting, geospatial analysis and large-scale administrative data.
