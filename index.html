About me

I am an economist and policy analyst with experience in migration, labour markets, inequality, applied econometrics, and public policy. I have worked with international organisations such as the OECD and the Inter-American Development Bank, as well as government institutions in Colombia. My work explores how migration, displacement, and economic shocks affect social protection, labour market outcomes, and development.

My technical background includes econometrics, geospatial analysis, forecasting, big data methods, and machine learning. I work with administrative data, survey microdata, and large-scale statistical systems.

Research

  • Migration shocks and preferences for redistribution in Colombia (working paper in progress).
  • Colorism, race and solidarity: heterogeneous effects of Venezuelan migration on attitudes toward welfare and redistribution.
  • Internal displacement and urban integration: evidence from Latin America and the Caribbean.
